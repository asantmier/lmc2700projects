About Me

Hi, I'm Allen! I'm a third-year computational media (Media/Games) student at Georgia Tech looking to hone my software engineering skills.

I'm primarily interested in developing and designing video games, but I enjoy working with computers in all aspects and can tackle an project that piques my interest. All of my skills are self-taught and I'm always looking to learn new languages and software to expand my skillset. More recently I've begun studying 3D modeling, shaders, and programming with XR.

My most important goals are to have fun with everything I do and make high quality products that people will enjoy!
